Solutions

A solution range for
effortless hospitality

Use a single QJumper product on its own, or bundle several for a fully unified operation. Every solution is built on the same platform, so everything stays in sync — menu, pricing, payments, and reporting.

Solution 01

Self-service kiosk

Let customers browse the menu at their own pace, customise orders without a queue, and pay without waiting for counter service. Kiosks consistently lift average order value through clear visuals and smart upsell prompts.

  • Full-menu browsing with images and modifiers
  • Built-in upsell and combo suggestions
  • Card and contactless payment at the kiosk
  • Accessibility-friendly flows and language options
  • Orders flow directly to kitchen and POS in real time
Solution 02

Branded mobile app

A fully branded app for your venue or group. Build direct customer relationships, drive repeat visits through loyalty and notifications, and keep the customer experience on your channel — not a third-party marketplace.

  • Your branding, colours, and menu — not a marketplace
  • Integrated loyalty, rewards, and offers
  • Order-ahead with pickup and delivery options
  • Push notifications for new menus and promotions
  • Customer profiles that enrich your reporting
Solution 03

Web ordering

Take online orders directly through your own website — no marketplace commissions, no middle layer between you and your customer. Perfect for venues that already have web traffic or want to keep delivery margins healthy.

  • Zero-commission ordering on your own domain
  • Mobile-responsive, fast-loading checkout
  • Integrates with delivery providers when you want it to
  • Unified menu and pricing with every other channel
  • Simple embed for existing websites
Solution 04

QR table ordering

Customers scan, order, and pay from their table. Reduces queues at the bar, frees up staff for service rather than order-taking, and lifts throughput during peak hours without adding headcount.

  • Scan-to-order from any phone — no app download
  • Table-aware: orders route to the right section
  • Pay at the table with card, Apple Pay, Google Pay
  • Split bills, tabs, and tipping built in
  • Ideal for high-volume bars, pubs, and events
Solution 05

Purpose-built POS

A point of sale designed for hospitality, not retail. Fast to learn, resilient under pressure, and fully connected to every other QJumper channel — so orders from the kiosk, app, and web all land in the same queue your team is running.

  • Hospitality-specific order flow and modifiers
  • Offline-tolerant — keeps taking orders if the network drops
  • Works with the payment terminals you already have
  • Multi-site menu and pricing managed centrally
  • Staff permissions, shift reports, and cash management
Solution 06

Reports & dashboards

Operational data at your fingertips. See real-time sales, order trends, channel mix, labour ratios, and peak-hour performance — with numbers that match across every view because they all come from the same platform.

  • Real-time sales and order volumes across sites
  • Channel breakdown: kiosk vs app vs web vs counter
  • Product performance and menu-engineering reports
  • Peak-hour heatmaps to plan rostering
  • Export to CSV, PDF, or push to your BI tool
