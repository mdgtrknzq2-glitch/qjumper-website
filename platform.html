The Platform

One platform behind
effortless hospitality

QJumper is designed to simplify hospitality operations. Instead of juggling a patchwork of disconnected tools, venues run ordering, payments, reporting, and customer interactions through a single, reliable system.

Why a platform, not a toolkit

Most hospitality software today is a stitched-together collection of apps — a POS from one vendor, kiosks from another, a separate ordering app, and a reporting dashboard that doesn't quite match any of them. Every integration point is a seam, and seams fail at the worst possible moment: a full house on a Saturday night.

How QJumper is different

Designed end-to-end, deployed as one.

Every part of QJumper is built to work with every other part. Your kiosk knows what's out of stock because your POS does. Your reports reconcile to the cent because orders never leave the platform. Nothing is bolted on — it's the same platform, presented in whichever surface your customers and staff need.

What the platform gives you

Four capability pillars that underpin every QJumper deployment.

Unified Operations

Orders placed on a kiosk, mobile app, web, QR table, or counter all flow into the same order queue. Payments, modifiers, discounts, and loyalty apply identically across channels — your team learns one system and runs everything.

Operational Clarity

Real-time dashboards surface what's happening right now across every channel and every site. Compare lunch vs. dinner, kiosk vs. counter, this week vs. last — with numbers that always agree because they come from the same source.

Scalable Architecture

Start with a single site and one product — add kiosks, web ordering, more locations, and advanced reporting as you grow. QJumper is designed for operators that run one café today and fifty locations in five years' time.

Integrations

Connect to the hospitality tools you already trust — delivery platforms, payment providers, accounting systems, and loyalty programmes. QJumper plays well with others, so you keep your existing stack wherever it's working.

Built to perform under pressure

Hospitality systems get judged on their worst day, not their average one. QJumper is architected for peak service — Friday dinner, matchday, Sunday brunch — so the platform stays predictable when everything else is chaotic.

  • Offline-tolerant order taking at the point of sale
  • Independent channels so one surface can't take the others down
  • Transparent status and live monitoring across every site
  • Upgrades that roll out without interrupting service

Operator-friendly from day one

The platform was designed by people who've run real hospitality businesses. That shows up in small ways and big ones — from the shape of the order screen to how multi-site pricing is managed — making QJumper faster to train and easier to live with than generic retail tools.

  • Hospitality-specific workflows, not a repurposed retail POS
  • Menu, pricing, and rules managed centrally — pushed everywhere
  • Clear, readable reports rather than spreadsheet dumps
  • Real people on support, not tiered call-centre tickets
