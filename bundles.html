Bundles

Start simple.
Scale without re-platforming.

QJumper bundles are a straightforward way to buy the platform. Pick what fits today — we'll size and price it to your venue — and add capability as your business grows. No module shuffling, no re-implementation.

How our bundles work

Every bundle is the same underlying platform — the difference is which surfaces (kiosk, POS, mobile, web, table, reports) are switched on. You can start with one surface and add others later without migrating data, retraining staff on a new system, or renegotiating a contract.

Why we quote, not list prices

Hospitality pricing should fit the venue.

Every operator is a little different — number of sites, transaction volume, peak hours, hardware already in place. Rather than forcing you into a pre-printed tier that doesn't match, we quote against your actual operation, so the number you see is the number that's right for you.
